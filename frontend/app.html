About TrueVision

TrueVision is an advanced forensic AI engine designed combat digital misinformation, specifically deepfakes and manipulated media.

Utilizing a robust tri-model ensemble (CNN mapping, CViT boundary detection, and ETCNN texture analysis), TrueVision provides law enforcement and analysts with near real-time, forensic-grade verdicts on image and video integrity.

Notice

While our ensemble is highly accurate, TrueVision AI can make mistakes. It is built to assist rather than replace human judgment. Please consistently verify high-stakes analysis with certified digital forensics experts.
