Extracts a 3D topographic mesh of faces to ensure consistent lighting, depth, and spatial geometry.

Input & Boundaries

Supported Formats

  • Images: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
  • Videos: .mp4, .avi, .mov

Current Boundaries

  • AI-generated videos (Veo, Sora, Runway) — no face-swap artifacts.
  • Heavily compressed videos — artifacts destroyed.
  • Faces smaller than 40×40 pixels — too blurry.
  • Non-face manipulations (body, background only).
